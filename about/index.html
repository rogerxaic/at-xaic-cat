Hobbies @ NPOs

Over the past few years I’ve found that a great way to gather around people with the same interests is to join (or create!) a non-profit organization.

During INSA I joined:

  • Graines d’images –the school’s photography association– as a photographer
  • 24 heures –a music festival & bike race– for the association’s CIO department

On my 3rd year, a friend made me discover hackathons. We went to Junction together (won a 2nd prize 🎉). The whole experience inspired me to:

  • Create HackLyon with a bunch of friends
  • Join Junction as an organizer during my Erasmus semester in Helsinki
